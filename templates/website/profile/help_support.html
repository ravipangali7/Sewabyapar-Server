Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to be logged in and have a shipping address.

How do I track my order?

Go to "My Orders" in your profile to view all your orders and their current status.

How do I book a taxi?

Go to the Taxi section, select your route, choose a date and time, and complete your booking.

How can I submit feedback or complain?

Go to your profile and click on "Feedback/Complain" to submit your feedback or complaint.
